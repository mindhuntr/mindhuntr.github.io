title = 'Creature of Eyes'
date = '2026-06-21T10:01:48+05:30'
description = 'A poem' 
tags = ['musings']
+++

Thrown into a world \
Where sight deceives \
Creatures of eyes \
Sought reprieve 

Things that were known \
Through the light of their gaze \
Seemed as many \
As the stars ablaze 

They lived in peace \
And loved to name \
A world they saw \
Where nothing was ever the same 

Yet among them\
A loansome creature was born \
With an eye turned inward \
A monster in form

Cursed they said\
The creature had been \
For it could weep and love \
All it had ever seen 

Things it knew \
Through the light of its gaze \
Seemed to be many \
Yet one and the same

So it lived in fear \
And abhorred to name \
A world it saw \
That words could never claim

Then what was real \
The creature could not tell \
For the eye turned within \
Had cast a morbid spell 

The gift of sight \
It did not now desire \
Since all it saw \
Mirrored its mire 

It wandered far away \
Searching for a cure \
That would make the inward eye \
See the outside lure

But the creature accursed \
Could never forget \
That the eye and the world \
Had always met
